Hi there. Meet Jissel.

Jissel is currently an undergraduate at Swarthmore College, a small liberal arts school near Philadelphia. She is studying History and Religion and intends to honors major in History, as terrifying as that may be.

She also intends on going to grad school to pursue a doctorate in History with a specialization in modern European history. The history of empire, particuarly French and British empire, excites her to no ends. In her free time, however, she enjoys playing bass, imagining the world of archives, and learning as much as she can about the digital humanities!
